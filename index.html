Artificial Orbit Simulation


This page is a small <canvas> experiment who simulates the gravity around the Earth. You can control a tiny ship with the arrow keys of your keyboard . So, it's easy to understand how works the placement into orbit around a planet.

You can choose which object of the scene becomes the referencial for the camera :

  • The ship
  • The Earth
  • Or the Universe (in this mode the background will doesn't move)
